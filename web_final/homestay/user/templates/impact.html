What does women's empowerment mean for the communities?
 When we say we support women empowerment in Nepal,
what does this mean exactly? Most of the Homestay
owners are women and housewives. The CommunityHomestay.com
platform enables women and their families in local communities
all over Nepal to generate a sustainable livelihood from responsible
tourism. We are coaching these women in Homestay Management, Hospitality,
Basic English and we are providing training in many other areas such as
teaching them how to interact with their foreign guests and exchange their
ideas and culture with visitors.
